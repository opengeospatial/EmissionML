[[req_emission_event]]
[requirement]
====
[%metadata]
identifier:: /req/datamodel/core/emission-event
part:: This requirement defines the Emission Event.
part:: Each Emission Event shall have a mandatory `endTime` of type TemporalBound.
part:: `hasEndTimeBasis` SHALL be present when `endTime.status = known` and SHALL be absent otherwise.
part:: `hasEndTimeEvidence` is not applicable when `endTime.status` is `unknown` or `ongoing`.
====
