[[req_temporal_bound]]
[requirement]
====
[%metadata]
identifier:: /req/datamodel/core/temporal-bound
part:: Each Temporal Bound shall have a mandatory `status` attribute of type TemporalStatus and a conditional `time` attribute of type TM_Instant.
part:: Constraint C1: `time` SHALL be present if and only if `status = known`.
part:: Constraint C2: `ongoing` applies to `endTime` only. A `startTime` SHALL have `status = known` or `status = unknown`.
====
