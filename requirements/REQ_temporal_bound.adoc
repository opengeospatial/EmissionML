[[req_temporal_bound]]
[requirement]
====
[%metadata]
identifier:: /req/datamodel/core/temporal-bound
part:: Each Temporal Bound shall have a mandatory `status` attribute of type TemporalStatus and a conditional `time` attribute of type TM_Instant.
part:: `time` SHALL be provided when `status = known`. `time` SHALL NOT be provided (i.e., the attribute is omitted, not null) when `status` is `unknown` or `ongoing`.
part:: `startTime.status` SHALL be `known`. `ongoing` and `unknown` apply to `endTime` only.
====
